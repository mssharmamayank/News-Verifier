About NewsVerifier.io

NewsVerifier is an AI-powered fake news detection platform built to help users quickly verify whether a news claim is real, misleading, or fake. It is designed for students, journalists, researchers, and everyday users who want to avoid misinformation.

⭐ What this website does

  • Shows the latest verified Indian news from trusted sources.
  • Allows users to submit any news headline, text, or article link for verification.
  • Uses a custom AI model to highlight suspicious statements and missing evidence.
  • Provides an explanation so users understand why a news may be fake or misleading.

🧠 How our AI verification works

  • User submits a news headline / paragraph / article link.
  • System extracts key claims and fetches supporting references from trusted online sources.
  • Our AI model compares the article with verified facts and cross-checks inconsistencies.
  • Model generates a detailed result: Real, Misleading, or Fake with reasoning.

📊 Model Accuracy & Performance

  • Trained on 10,000+ real and fake news samples.
  • Optimised for English Indian news headlines & articles.
  • Current classification accuracy: 85% – 90% (expected to improve with more training data).
  • Bias-reduction and fact-confidence score included to avoid false predictions.

✔ Where NewsVerifier can be useful

  • Students verifying viral WhatsApp forwards.
  • Journalism and media research projects.
  • General users who want to confirm news before sharing.
  • Teachers who want to promote digital literacy.

NewsVerifier is a college research project to promote responsible news consumption. It does not replace professional fact-checking agencies but assists users in making informed decisions online.
